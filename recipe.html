Strawberry Chia Seed Jam

Yummy jam without all the sugar!

Ingredients

5 cups strawberries, washed and cut in half, tops removed
1/2 cup xylitol
1 Tablespoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar or vanilla

Instructions

  • Using the stovetop, cook berries and xylitol with half an inch of water in a large pot. Bring to a rapid simmer for 20 minutes, then reduce and slowly simmer until liquid is reduced and berries have thickened.
  • Once berries are cooked, taste a bit on a spoon. Add more xylitol if you want it sweeter.
  • Stir in lemon juice, balsalmic vinegar, and chia seeds. (You won't notice the chia seeds in the finished product because of the seeds in the strawberries.)
  • Ladle into jars, and store in the fridge for up to 2 weeks, the freezer for up to a year, or use the traditional hot-water canning method to preserve jam for your pantry.
